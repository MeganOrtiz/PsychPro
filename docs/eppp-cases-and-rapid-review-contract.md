# EPPP Clinical Cases and Rapid Review Contract

Create both Clinical Integration Cases and Rapid Review assets, but build them
in this order:

1. Part 1 Knowledge Domains
2. Part 2 Skills Domains

Do not mix these assets into the main PsychPro course library.

## Clinical Integration Cases

Part 1 cases should be created first for these domains:

- Biological Bases of Behavior
- Cognitive-Affective Bases of Behavior
- Social and Cultural Bases of Behavior
- Growth and Lifespan Development
- Assessment and Diagnosis
- Treatment, Intervention, Prevention, and Supervision
- Research Methods and Statistics
- Ethical, Legal, and Professional Issues

Each case should include:

- Case title
- Exam part: `part1`
- Domain
- Competency tags
- Client/context description
- Presenting issue
- Key decision point
- Questions
- Answer choices
- Correct answer
- Rationales for correct and incorrect answers
- Difficulty
- Review tags

After Part 1 cases are loaded, create Part 2 cases for:

- Assessment and Intervention Skills
- Consultation and Supervision Skills
- Scientific Thinking and Evidence Use
- Professional Ethics and Legal Decision-Making
- Communication, Relationships, and Diversity
- Clinical Reasoning and Applied Judgment

## Rapid Review

Part 1 Rapid Review assets should be created first for the same eight Part 1
knowledge domains.

Each Rapid Review asset should include:

- Review title
- Exam part: `part1`
- Domain
- Core concepts
- Common EPPP traps
- Compare/contrast frames
- Quick retrieval prompts
- Mini application checks
- Related lessons

After Part 1 Rapid Review is loaded, create Part 2 Rapid Review assets for the
six Part 2 skills domains.

## Separation Rules

- Part 1 teaches and reinforces what to know.
- Part 2 trains what to do with that knowledge.
- Clinical cases should test applied reasoning.
- Rapid Review should be concise final-pass review, not full chapters and not a
  flashcard replacement.
- Every asset must be tagged as Part 1 or Part 2 before upload.
