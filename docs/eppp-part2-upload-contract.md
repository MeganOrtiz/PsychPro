# EPPP Part 2 Upload Contract

Part 2 content belongs inside the EPPP Mastery Suite only.

Do not upload Part 2 lessons into the main PsychPro course library or into the
Part 1 knowledge-domain categories.

## Destination

- Primary route: `/eppp/skills`
- Suite tab: `/eppp/suite/part-2-skills`

## Required Category Format

Every Part 2 lesson should use one of these category prefixes:

- `Part 2: Assessment and Intervention Skills`
- `Part 2: Consultation and Supervision Skills`
- `Part 2: Scientific Thinking and Evidence Use`
- `Part 2: Professional Ethics and Legal Decision-Making`
- `Part 2: Communication, Relationships, and Diversity`
- `Part 2: Clinical Reasoning and Applied Judgment`

The app also recognizes `EPPP Skills:` as a valid prefix.

## Content Shape

Each Part 2 topic should be treated as an applied skill lesson, not a Part 1
knowledge chapter.

Recommended topic shape:

- Skill domain
- Skill area
- Scenario-based lesson
- Application drill
- Case-based or applied questions
- Feedback and reasoning review

## Separation Rules

- Part 1 content teaches what to know.
- Part 2 content trains what to do with that knowledge.
- Part 1 readiness and domain mastery should remain Part 1-only until a separate
  Part 2 readiness model is intentionally designed.
- Main PsychPro content stays on the main site.
- EPPP content stays inside the EPPP Mastery Suite.
