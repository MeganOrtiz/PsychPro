-ECT 
Electroconvulsive Therapy 
Treatment that sends electric current through the brain 
Electrodes attached at precise locations and signals administered 
Evokes seizures to "reset" the brain 
Can treat: 
Depression 
Mania 
Catatonia 
Agitation and aggression in those with dementia 
Risks: 
Memory loss 
Difficulty learning after 
Nausea/headache/fatigue/confusion 
May be more effective than most medications

Major Depressive Disorder 
OCD 
Cigarette smoking

Repeated pulses
