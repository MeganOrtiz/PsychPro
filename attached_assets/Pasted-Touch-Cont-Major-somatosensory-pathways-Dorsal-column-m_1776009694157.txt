-Olfactory receptors

-Olfaction in the brain

Chemical Senses: Taste 
-Gustation 
Sense of taste 
-Function: Tell us to swallow or spit something out 
Sweets are preferred beginning in infancy 
Evolutionary basis 
-Receptors 
Papillae on tongue 
Each taste bud has about 50 receptors 
-Pathway 
Mouth--> cranial nerves VII, IX and X--> solitary nucleus (medulla)--> ventral posterior nuclei (thalamus)--> inferoparietal lobe (primary and secondary gustatory cortex) 
-Clinical implications: 
Anosmia- inability to smell 
Cause: blow to the head that damages olfactory nerves 
Ageusia- inability to taste 
Rare due to many pathways carrying taste information
