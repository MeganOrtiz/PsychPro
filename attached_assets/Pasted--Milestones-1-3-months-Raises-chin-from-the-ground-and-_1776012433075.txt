-Reflexes 
Babinski 
When the bottom of the foot is stroked, the toes fan out 
Root 
When the baby's mouth is stroked, the baby opens its mouth 
Moro 
Will reach arms and palms up when there is a sudden loud noise or movement 
-Theory of Mind- ability to make inferences about another's representational states and to predict behavior accordingly 
2-3 years- children begin to become aware of other people's mental states and begin to understand that other people have different perceptions, emotions and desires 
4-5 years- children understand that another person's thoughts may be false and that people may act on their false beliefs 
5-+ years- children begin to recognize that peoples actions are not always consistent with true thoughts/feelings, recognize variances in interpretation 
-Attachment 
Human infants need a consistent nurturing relationship with one or more sensitive caregivers in order to develop into healthy individuals. Inadequate relationships are considered to contribute to abnormal behavior and in combination with other risk factors to psychopathology
