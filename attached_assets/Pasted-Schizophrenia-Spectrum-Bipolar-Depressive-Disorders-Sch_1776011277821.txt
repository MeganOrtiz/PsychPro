Schizophrenia Spectrum/Bipolar/Depressive Disorders 
 
Schizophrenia Spectrum and Other Psychotic Disorders 
-Abnormalities in 5 domains: 
Hallucinations  
Can involve any of the 5 senses 
Delusions 
Persecutory- belief in potential offenses by others 
Referential- gestures, comments and cutes directed at individual 
Grandiose- exceptional abilities 
Erotomanic- belief that everyone is in love with the individual 
Nihilistic- catastrophic event 
Somatic- bodily sensations 
Delusions must be bizarre and not contextually or culturally relevant 
Disorganized thinking/speech 
Derailment or loose associations 
Tangentiality 
Abnormal motor behavior (catatonia included) 
Problems with goal directed behavior 
Catatonia- decreased reactivity to environment 
Negative symptoms 
Diminished emotional expression 
Avolition- diminished motivation and self-initiation for purposeful activities
