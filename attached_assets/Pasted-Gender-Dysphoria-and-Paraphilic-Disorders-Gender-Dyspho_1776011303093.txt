Gender Dysphoria and Paraphilic Disorders 
 
Gender Dysphoria Disorder 
-Controversy over sex and gender 
Sex/sexual in DSM- biological indicators for male and female 
Gender- sociocultural 
 
-Gender dysphoria- distress accompanying incongruence between experienced/express gender and assigned gender 
Criterion A: incongruence for at least 6 mo 
Other criteria under A 
Criterion B: must cause significant impairment and distress 
Has specifiers 
-Other Specified Gender Dysphoria Disorder 
-Nonspecified Gender Dysphoria Disorder 
 
Paraphilic Disorders 
-Paraphilia- any intense and persistent sexual interest in other than interest in genital stimulation or preparatory fondling when phenotypically normal, physically mature, consenting human partners 
Activities or targets 
 
-Voyeuristic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, observing unsuspecting person naked, disrobing or engaging in sexual activity 
 Can be a voyeur but non-disclosing in that they have been "caught" watching but do not report impairment or distress 
-Exhibitionistic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, exposing ones genitals to an unsuspecting person\ 
Can include prepubescent children, adults or both 
Consider pedophilia 
-Frotteuristic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, touching or rubbing against unsuspecting/non-consenting person 
-Sexual Masochism Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, act of being humiliated, beaten, bound, or otherwise made to suffer 
Specifier: with asphyxiophilia 
-Sexual Sadism Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, physical or psychological suffering of another individual 
-Pedophilic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, arousing fantasies, sexual urges or behaviors involving sexual activity with a prepubescent child or children 
Criterion C: Individual is at least 16 and at least 5 years older than child?? 
Age guideline for general onset of puberty 
-Fetishistic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, use of nonliving objects or a highly specific focus on nongenital body parts 
Criterion C: Not limited to certain articles of clothing used in cross dressing (as in transvestic disorder) 
-Transvestic Disorder 
Criterion A: Period of 6 months, cross dressing 
Specifiers: 
With fetishism- fabrics. Materials or garments 
With autogynephilia- thoughts or images of self as a woman  
-Other Specified Paraphilic Disorder 
-Unspecified Paraphilic Disorder
